AxonPlay Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Introduction

AxonPlay ("we", "our", or "the app") is a retro game emulator for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

AxonPlay is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect usage analytics or telemetry
  • We do not use tracking technologies or advertising SDKs
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

Data Stored Locally

All app data is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it:

  • ROM files: Game files you load are read from your device storage
  • Save states: Game progress is saved locally on your device
  • Settings: Your preferences (controls, display filters, etc.) are stored locally

This data is only accessible by the app and is removed if you uninstall AxonPlay.

Permissions

AxonPlay may request the following Android permissions:

  • Storage access: Required to load ROM files and save game progress
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback on virtual controls

Third-Party Services

AxonPlay does not integrate any third-party services, analytics platforms, ad networks, or social media SDKs.

Children's Privacy

AxonPlay does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages as no personal information is gathered.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
