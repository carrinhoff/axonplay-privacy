AxonPlay Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Introduction

AxonPlay ("we", "our", or "the app") is a retro game emulator for Android and iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • ROM files: Game files you load are read from your device storage
  • Save states: Game progress is saved locally on your device
  • Settings & preferences: Display filters, controls, theme selection, and other preferences
  • Button layout: Custom gamepad button positions

This data is only accessible by the app and is removed if you uninstall AxonPlay.

Data We Collect & Transmit

AxonPlay uses the following services that may process data externally:

AI Game Assistant (Google Gemini)

When you use the AI Game Assistant feature, a screenshot of your current game screen and your text question are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. This data is used solely to generate a contextual game hint. Google processes this data according to their Privacy Policy. You can choose not to use this feature.

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used (e.g., which features are popular, crash reports). This includes anonymized usage data such as screen views, feature interactions, and device information. No personally identifiable information is collected. See Firebase Privacy.

Cloud Sync (Google Drive)

If you enable Cloud Sync, your save files are uploaded to your personal Google Drive account. This requires Google Sign-In. We only access the app-specific folder in your Drive and do not read any other files. You can disable Cloud Sync at any time in Settings.

RetroAchievements

If you enable RetroAchievements integration, your RetroAchievements username and API key are stored locally and used to communicate with the RetroAchievements API to track game achievements. Your credentials are never shared with any other service.

Homebrew Game Discovery

The Discover section fetches free homebrew game listings from the Homebrew Hub API and downloads game files from GitHub. No personal data is sent during this process.

Subscriptions & Purchases

AxonPlay offers optional in-app purchases and subscriptions ("GBA Pro") through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google — we do not collect or store any payment information.

  • GBA Pro Monthly: Auto-renewing monthly subscription
  • GBA Pro Annual: Auto-renewing annual subscription
  • Premium Themes: One-time purchase

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your device's store settings (App Store or Google Play). No refunds will be provided for the unused portion of any subscription period.

Free features (emulation, save states, ROM management) remain fully available without any subscription.

Permissions

AxonPlay may request the following permissions:

  • Storage access: Required to load ROM files and save game progress
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback on virtual controls
  • Internet access: Required for AI hints, homebrew discovery, cloud sync, RetroAchievements, and subscription management
  • Camera/Gyroscope: Optional, used for gyroscope/tilt controls in supported games

Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data (ROMs, saves, settings) is deleted when you uninstall the app. Cloud Sync data stored in your Google Drive can be deleted through your Google Drive settings. Firebase Analytics data is retained in anonymized, aggregated form. To request deletion of any data associated with your use of the app, contact us at the email below.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR in the EU, CCPA in California), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

AxonPlay does not collect any personal data from users, including children under 13. The AI Game Assistant only processes in-game screenshots (game visuals) — no personal user data, photos, or camera input is ever captured or transmitted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
